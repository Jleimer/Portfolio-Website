About Me

The beginning of my design process is rooted from my imagination. I find myself highly influenced by science fiction, such as robotics and stories of mad scientists. I integrate many non-traditional materials and found objects, challenging myself to include these materials without making them permanent. Many of my pieces I consider to be sculptural, but they are very interactive and influence people to want to pick up the piece and interact with it. I begin my pieces with an idea and a starting point, and I let my imagination take over the design process, letting the piece to develop as my imagination begins to build as I work. This allows me to work out and develop each aspect of a piece with attention and detail as I see it develop. When a piece is finished it brings a smile to my face seeing something that was an imagined story in my head to becoming something I can touch and use.
